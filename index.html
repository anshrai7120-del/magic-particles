Hand‑Controlled Particle System Guide

Use your hand(s) in front of the webcam to control the visualization.

Single Hand Gestures

  • L‑Shape: Toggle Dynamic Mode
  • Pinch: Expansion / Contraction
  • Victory: Change Shape
  • Pinky: Change Color Template
  • Point: Wireframe Mode
  • Thumbs Up: Toggle Planets
  • Fist: Toggle Rotation
  • Open Palm: Color Pulse

Two Hand Gestures

  • Move Hands: Camera Zoom
  • Two Fists Close: Hyper Mode
  • Two Thumbs Up: Reset

Camera permission required
